Pulled from https://ftp.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pub/taxonomy on 2020-06-23
	created with ndi_createGenBankControlledVocabulary.m

Environmental samples with "environmental sample" or "Sample" in the Scientific_name are removed

The file is gzipped and has columns:

Scientific_Name      - the GenBank standard scientific name, ends in a <tab>  (these are alphabetized)
GenBank_Common_Name   - the common name as defined by GenBank, ends in a <tab>
Synonyms             - Any synonyms, items separated separated by a ', ' and ends in a <tab>
Other_Common_Name     - Any other common names, items separated by a ', ' and ends in a <newline>
