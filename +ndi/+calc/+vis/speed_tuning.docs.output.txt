Produces a document of type 'ndi_calculation_speedtuning'

Superclasses: ndi_document, ndi_document_app, speed_tuning

The primary purpose of the ndi_calculation_speedtuning document is to hold the
results of a speed_tuning fit. The speed tuning fit document has the following
fields:
